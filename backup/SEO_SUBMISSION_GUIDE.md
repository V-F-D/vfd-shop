# 🚀 How to Submit Your Site to Google Search Console

To ensure "Victory Fashion Design" appears in search results immediately, follow these steps.

## Step 1: Sign in to Google Search Console

1. Go to **[search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)**.
2. Sign in with your Google Account.

## Step 2: Add Your Property

1. Click **"Add Property"** (top left dropdown).
2. Choose **"URL Prefix"** (Easier method).
3. Enter your full URL: `https://vfd-shop.vercel.app` (or your custom domain if you have one).
4. Click **Continue**.

## Step 3: Verify Ownership

Google needs to know you own the site.

1. Choose **"HTML Tag"** method.
2. Copy the meta tag provided (e.g., `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="..." />`).
3. Send this tag to me (or paste it in `index.html` <head> section manually).
4. Once added and deployed, click **Verify** in the console.

## Step 4: Submit Sitemap

Once verified:

1. In the left sidebar, click **"Sitemaps"**.
2. Under "Add a new sitemap", enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **Submit**.

## ✅ Success!

Google will now process your pages.

- **Immediate:** Google knows your pages exist.
- **2-4 Days:** You should start seeing your pages in Google Search for "Victory Fashion Design".

**Tip:** Do the same for **Bing Webmaster Tools** to capture the other ~10% of traffic!
